Yinzer13

Pittsburgh

This little bakery is such a welcome addition to the atmosphere and offerings Lawrenceville provides! The owners are from France and have not Americanized their pastries or breads! You can get a sandwhich or bread or pastries and eat it there on the very few tables provided. At lunchtime you can imagine it getting rather crowded and tables being scarce!
