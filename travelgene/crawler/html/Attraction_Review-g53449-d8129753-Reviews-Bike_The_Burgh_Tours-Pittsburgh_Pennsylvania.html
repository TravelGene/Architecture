    Description:

    Bike the Burgh Tours offers city tours through Pittsburgh. Visitors as well...

    Bike the Burgh Tours offers city tours through Pittsburgh. Visitors as well as locals will be introduced to historical, architectural and cultural facts about the landmarks seen along the way. Exploring the city on a bike allows one to discover very special sites that cannot be seen on regular bus or Ducky tours while getting some healthy physical activity.
